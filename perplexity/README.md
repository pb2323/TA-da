# Perplexity Sonar (Research & Verification)

Grounded research and citation for concept cards and misconception detection.

## Responsibilities

- **“Show sources”** — Attach citations to concept cards and explanations
- **Misconception detection** — Use Sonar to validate or flag common misconceptions in student questions and instructor content
